Journals

This is one of my older journals from highschool, at this time I usually doodled on lined paper instead of taking notes.

This is one of my newer journals I took on a road trip,during my time at community college. I tried experimenting with fashion designs for characters.

This journal is from highschool as well. I think this might be the first year I participated in Inktober. Inktober is an everyday drawing challenge taking place in October where you make an ink drawing everyday based on a set of one word prompts.

A journal of me testing how to use ink wash techniques for a class.

A small lined notepad I keep for sketching. Lots of the time my parents will find abandoned notebooks in their offices to give to me.

Another mini lined journal, this one I mostly keep at home on my desk for lists or random doodles.

This is one of my mixed media journals. It can handle acrylic, watercolor and alcohol markers pretty well. I try not to get picky about making these nicer journals art only look "perfect". Journals are a place to experiment and fail.

A sketch book I carry around in my school backpack. I use it for notes in class along with mindlessly doodling what I might be feeling.

I've been drawing for long time on and off. Ever since I was a kid, the first things I ever drew were rats and foxes on the back of homework assignments. I still have a long way to go in my art journey but I'm happy to look back and see all the progress I've made over the years.
